In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

This dataset was created to serve researchers interested in classifying hadiths as authentic or weak. It was compiled from the Seven Books, which are Sahih Muslim, Sahih Bukhari, Ibn Majah, Al-Nasa'i, Al-Tirmidhi, and Al-Mawdu'at by Ibn al-Jawzi. The size of this dataset contains 10,000 hadiths and approximately 7,000 narrators. The authentic and weak hadiths were compiled in proportions from each book. The seven books were selected based on the specialists in the Hadith Department at the Faculty of Usul al-Din and Da'wah, Al-Azhar University in Assiut, Egypt. The dataset was reviewed by specialists in the science of hadith at Al-Azhar University in Assiut, Egypt.

There are five files.

1- hadith_dataset: This file contains all 10,000 hadiths after they were processed and reviewed by specialists from Al-Azhar University.

2- isandSahihHadith: Contains hadiths with authentic chains of narration (isnad).

3- isandDaifHadith: Contains hadiths with weak chains of narration (isnad).

4-SahihMatn: Contains hadiths with authentic texts (sahih matn).

5- DaifMatn: Contains hadiths with weak texts (da'if matn).
